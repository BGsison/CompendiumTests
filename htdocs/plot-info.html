The Calamity Box is a medium‐sized, antique music box ornamented with gold and three gems of different colors; one green, one blue, and one pink. The gems represent three key traits, each relating to a member of a certain trio whose fate is predetermined by a "long‐lost" ancient prophecy. What calamity could it possibly bring?
